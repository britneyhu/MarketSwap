Embark on a pixelated medieval adventure to you trade your way to success. As you navigate the town and terrain, keep your eyes peeled for special items that can help you unlock the coveted goal items. Are you ready to take on the challenge and claim your rightful place as a master trader?

Game Created by Britney Hu     |     Most of the art in this game is free and open source content from OpenGameArt     |     Full Art Credits

Controls

To move: WASD

Interact with items and shops: Mouse

How to Play

Talk to shop owners and take note of what items they want to trade

Find items throughout the map or trade your way to them

Collecting special items on the ground adds 30 seconds and earning a goal item adds 60 seconds

Obtain all the goal items before the timer runs out to win!
